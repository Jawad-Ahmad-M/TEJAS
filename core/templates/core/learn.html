What is TEJAS?

Revolutionizing Tender Evaluation

TEJAS (Tender Evaluation and Judgment Assessment System) is a cutting-edge platform designed to transform how organizations evaluate and process tender documents. Built on advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies, TEJAS automates the traditionally time-consuming and error-prone process of tender evaluation.

Our system combines state-of-the-art natural language processing, computer vision, and anomaly detection algorithms to provide comprehensive analysis of tender submissions, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and fairness throughout the evaluation process.

How TEJAS Works

A seamless process from upload to evaluation

Step 1: Document Upload & Preprocessing

Upload tender documents in any format (PDF, Word, Excel, images). Our system automatically extracts text, tables, and structured data using advanced OCR technology. Documents are categorized and indexed for efficient processing.

  • Multi-format support (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, images)
  • Batch upload capability for multiple documents
  • Automatic language detection and translation
  • Secure cloud storage with encryption

Step 2: AI-Powered Information Extraction

Advanced NLP algorithms identify and extract key information including pricing, specifications, timelines, qualifications, and compliance statements. The system understands context and relationships between different data points.

  • Automated extraction of pricing and cost structures
  • Technical specification parsing and comparison
  • Qualification and credential verification
  • Timeline and milestone identification

Step 3: Anomaly Detection & Validation

Machine learning models trained on thousands of tenders analyze submissions for irregularities, inconsistencies, and potential red flags. The system cross-references data points and validates against historical patterns.

  • Price anomaly detection (unusually high or low bids)
  • Document authenticity verification
  • Compliance gap identification
  • Risk scoring and fraud indicators

Step 4: Evaluation & Reporting

Generate comprehensive evaluation reports with scoring matrices, comparisons, and recommendations. All decisions are backed by data and include complete audit trails for transparency and accountability.

  • Automated scoring based on predefined criteria
  • Side-by-side tender comparisons
  • Detailed recommendation reports
  • Complete audit trail documentation
